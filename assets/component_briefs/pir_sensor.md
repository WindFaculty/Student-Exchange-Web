# PIR Motion Sensor (HC-SR501)

The HC‑SR501 passive infrared (PIR) sensor detects motion by measuring infrared radiation. It requires 5–20 V supply and outputs a digital high level when motion is detected. The delay and sensitivity are adjustable; the sensing range is up to 7 m with a field‑of‑view under 120°【152121044289515†L104-L115】【152121044289515†L98-L139】.

- **Supply voltage**: 5–20 V
- **Output**: Digital high when motion detected
- **Sensing range**: up to 7 m
- **Field of view**: <120°
- **Adjustable delay**: 5–200 s
