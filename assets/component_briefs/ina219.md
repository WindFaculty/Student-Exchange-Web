# INA219 Current Sensor

The INA219 is a bidirectional current/power monitor with I²C interface. It measures shunt voltage and bus voltage up to 26 V, providing current and power calculations with high accuracy and programmable addresses. The device is widely used for energy monitoring projects【228639711661899†L100-L115】.

- **Bus voltage range**: 0–26 V
- **Current measurement**: Up to ±3.2 A (depends on shunt resistor)
- **Power calculation**: Integrated multiplier
- **Interface**: I²C (up to 16 addresses)
- **Voltage**: 3.0–5.5 V
