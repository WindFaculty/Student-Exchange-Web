# HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor

The HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor measures distance by sending ultrasonic pulses and timing the echo. It operates at 5 V and can measure distances from about 2 cm to 400 cm with a typical accuracy of ±3 mm.

- **Supply voltage**: 5 V
- **Current draw**: ~15 mA
- **Range**: 2–400 cm
- **Interface**: Digital trigger/echo pins
