# SG90 Micro Servo

The SG90 is a popular micro servo motor offering approximately 180° rotation. It operates at 4.8–6 V and is controlled by a PWM signal. The servo provides around 1.8 kg·cm torque at 4.8 V and a speed of ~0.1 s per 60°【650206876400656†L69-L94】.

- **Supply voltage**: 4.8–6 V
- **Control signal**: PWM
- **Torque**: ~1.8 kg·cm at 4.8 V
- **Speed**: ~0.1 s/60° (4.8 V)
- **Rotation range**: 180°
