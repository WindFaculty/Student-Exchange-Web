# RC522 RFID Module

The RC522 RFID reader module is based on the NXP MFRC522 IC. It operates in the 13.56 MHz ISM band and supports SPI, I²C and UART communication. The module’s operating voltage ranges from 2.5 V to 3.3 V and it can communicate at up to 10 Mbps. Logic inputs are 5 V tolerant【624142837016568†L185-L191】.

- **Operating voltage**: 2.5–3.3 V (5 V tolerant inputs)
- **Frequency**: 13.56 MHz
- **Communication**: SPI, I²C, UART
- **Max data rate**: 10 Mbps
- **Current consumption**: 13–26 mA
