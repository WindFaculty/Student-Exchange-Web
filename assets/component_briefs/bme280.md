# BME280 Sensor

The BME280 is an integrated environmental sensor measuring temperature, humidity and barometric pressure. It communicates over I²C or SPI and includes an on‑board regulator and level shifter, allowing 3.3 V or 5 V operation【454565952017535†L53-L75】【454565952017535†L116-L118】.

- **Temperature range**: −40 °C to 85 °C (±1 °C accuracy)
- **Humidity range**: 0–100 % RH (±3 % accuracy)
- **Pressure range**: 300–1100 hPa (±1 hPa accuracy)
- **Interface**: I²C or SPI
- **Voltage**: 3.3 V (regulator allows 5 V supply)
