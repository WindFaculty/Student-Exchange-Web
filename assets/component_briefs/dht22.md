# DHT22 Temperature & Humidity Sensor

The DHT22 (AM2302) is a low‑cost digital sensor that measures temperature and relative humidity. It communicates via a single‑wire digital interface.

- **Temperature range**: –40 °C to 80 °C (±0.5 °C)
- **Humidity range**: 0–100 % RH (±2 %)
- **Voltage**: 3.3–6 V
- **Interface**: Single‑wire digital
